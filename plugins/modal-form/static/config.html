2 Where submissions go

Paste a Discord webhook URL for the channel that should receive answers, or pick one you saved in DWEEB.
How do I get a webhook URL?
  1. In Discord, open Server Settings → Integrations → Webhooks.
  2. Click New Webhook, choose the channel answers should land in.
  3. Click Copy Webhook URL and paste it above.
